#  Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

##  General Safety Tips
- Always verify the sender before clicking on links in emails or messages  
- Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible  
- Do not share OTPs, passwords, or personal details with anyone  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions  

##  UPI & Financial Safety
- Never approve unknown UPI payment requests or “collect requests”  
- Double-check the receiver’s UPI ID before sending money  
- Do not scan random QR codes received from unknown sources  
- Avoid clicking on links claiming rewards, cashback, or lottery winnings  

##  Awareness & Action
- Keep your devices updated with the latest security patches  
- Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store  
- Immediately report cyber fraud at https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 in case of financial fraud
