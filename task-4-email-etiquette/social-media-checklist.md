# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

##  Do’s
- Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts and comments  
- Think before you post — ensure your content reflects positively on you  
- Use strong privacy settings to protect your personal information  
- Share useful and meaningful content related to academics or skills  
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation  

##  Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information (address, phone number, passwords)  
- Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying  
- Avoid oversharing your personal life or emotions publicly  
- Do not accept friend requests from unknown or suspicious accounts
